About Me

Hi! My name is Oneisha Grant. I just turned 27 years old and I have a 4 year old son. I am a full time student with a passion for learning and growing in the digital world. I have been a freelance website designer for about 2 years now, but I grew tired of not being able to fully customize websites because I didn't know how to code.

Why I'm Taking This Course

I am taking this course to expand my knowledge of web development. Before, I was using platforms such as Wix and WordPress. While those sites do allow you to customize with HTML and CSS, I couldn't offer my clients much more customization than the pre-made templates, or the templates I could make myself. I wanted to make real websites. Since taking WebD 152 and starting this course, I have gained a deeper understanding of how websites work from the ground up. I can't believe I even know how to build a webpage from scratch! The most exciting part of these courses is overcoming things that once intimidated me. One day I hope to own my own media agency for people looking to build their brand image.
